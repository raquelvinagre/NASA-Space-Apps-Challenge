About Our App

The Baixo Vouga Lagunar, part of the Aveiro lagoon in Portugal, is a wetland region with rich biodiversity and significant agricultural activity. However, saline intrusion is degrading agricultural soils, leading to land abandonment and affecting the local economy.

We combined Sentinel L2A satellite data for the time period from January 1 to January 31, 2019, to visualize the average values of the NDVI, NDSI, and SI9 indices. The NDVI index is represented in the project as "vegetation index," the NDSI as "normalized salinity index," and the SI9 as "salinity level index." These indices allow us to visualize the areas most affected by salinity in the Baixo Vouga Lagunar region. For the development of the WebApp, we used Python and FastAPI.​
